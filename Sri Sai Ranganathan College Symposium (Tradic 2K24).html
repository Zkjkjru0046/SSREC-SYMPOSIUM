Welcome to the National Level Technical Symposium (Tradic 2K24)

Welcome to National Level Technical Symposium (Tradic 2K24), the national-level technical symposium hosted by Sri Sai Ranganathan Engineering College. This exciting event will feature insightful presentations that highlight the latest innovations in technology and research. Attendees will have the opportunity to participate in interactive workshops, where they can learn practical skills from industry experts. Additionally, the symposium offers an excellent platform for networking, connecting with peers, professionals, and academics. Whether you're presenting your work or attending as a guest, Tradic 2K24 promises a day of learning and collaboration. Join us for insightful presentations, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities at Sri Sai Ranganathan Engineering College.

Date: 4th October 2024

Time: 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Main Auditorium, SSREC

Registration Fee: ₹200 /-(per Team)
